2. Description of Service

REVELA is a geospatial and field operations tool designed to assist the BPLO in identifying, inspecting, and managing business registrations within the Municipality of Mataasnakahoy. The platform consists of:

  • A Mobile App: For authorized Field Inspectors to receive assignments, navigate to locations, document findings with notes and photos, and submit inspection reports.
  • A Web Portal: For authorized Administrators to manage the business registry, run geospatial analysis, assign tasks, review reports, and oversee system operations.
